## Problem

The P4 metronome VOL and VOICE required a Save + reboot to take effect, while the
LED colours and the accent toggle already applied live via `/live`. There are no
audio files — the voice is a synthesized sine tone burst (`render_burst`, pitch +
length from `metronome_voice_params`) and volume is an ES8311 codec register — so
nothing needs "pulling"; they were just consumed once at boot in
`metronome_audio_start`.

## Fix

- `metronome_audio.c/.h`: add `metronome_audio_set(volume, voice)` — re-set the codec
  volume and re-render the two tone bursts at runtime. No-op until `_ready()` (the
  codec/I2S only come up at boot when the metronome is enabled, so *enabling* a
  metronome that was off still needs a reboot; only vol/voice of a running metronome
  are live).
- `ks_web.cpp` `live_handler`: copy `metronome_volume` + `metronome_voice` into the
  running config and call `metronome_audio_set()`; mark the VOL/VOICE inputs
  `class="live"` so the client posts them on change (VOL debounced on input, VOICE on
  change — auto-wired by the existing `.live` handler).

## Notes

- Built + flashed to the P4. Validate on hardware: with the metronome enabled and
  audible, change VOL/VOICE in the web UI and confirm the click changes with no
  reboot.
- Concurrency: a click landing mid-re-render is at worst one slightly-off burst —
  fine for a user-driven config edit.
