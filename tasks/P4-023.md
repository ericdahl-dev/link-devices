## Goal

Richer metronome click: choose which subdivisions click (quarter, eighth, 16th),
each with its **own volume** and **swing**, so the click can carry a groove rather
than a flat pulse.

## Inspiration

JMK CLOCKstep:MULTI "Metronome Click" — quarter / eighth / 16th intervals, each
with independent volume and swing (https://jmkmusicpedals.com/products/clockstep-multi).

## Current state

We click once per beat with a bar-1 accent (P4-006), a voice preset + master
volume (P4-012), and swing lives on the clock outputs (P4-013), not the click.

## Scope

- Extend the pure `metronome` module: emit events per enabled subdivision (1 / 2 /
  4 per beat) with a per-subdivision volume + swing amount. Host-tested.
- Glue: `metronome_audio_click` gains a volume/level argument (codec volume per
  hit) so intervals can differ.
- Web UI: per-interval enable + volume (+ reuse the swing control shape).

## Deliverable

A swung 8th/16th click with the downbeat loudest; host-tested subdivision logic.
